About Ayushree Hospital

Welcome To Ayushree Hospital

The Ayushree Hospital, Pune, India, is the largest Non-Government Organization hospital in the Pune District of Maharashtra State. Run by Mr.Vilas Dange. The hospital is a 550-bedded, serving not only the people of the city itself, but also a large populace coming from the surrounding urban and rural areas.

The Ayushree Hospital also runs a secondary level Rural Hospital at Pune, which serves a rural population of about 68,000 people through a network of primary health centers. All the major clinical departments like Medicine, Surgery, Obstetrics, Gynecology, Pathology and Radiology.
